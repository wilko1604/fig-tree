The Fig Tree Sandbox

Why is this what it is?

This website is a sandbox site, rather than a portfolio. I aim to use this as a way to practice what i learn regarding HTML, CSS and Javascript. The content of this website is here for that exact reason. Content. I decided to use my builds to push me to build more refined creations while also giving myself a decent ground of what I can add to this website.

Fig Tree?

The title fig tree was written on a whim, having a fig tree infront of me did help the decision. Anyway, turns out I didnt hate having the words "fig tree" glaring at me everytime I edit the docments or reloaded the website. Hence, why the name stuck.

Well... Whats the content?

I'm glad you asked... Here I present to you at colection of home builds I have worked on. These builds have become a standard creative output in my life serving my aesthetic preferences while creating a more efficient and comfortable personal space. I've found I enjoy all the aspects of my builds from the thought and designing, to the construction to the satisfction of a completed product. None of the builds are masterpieces, they all have their quirks, and flaws reflecting a sort of DIY ethic tone in each build.
